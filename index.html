Who I Am

Driven By
More Than
Speed

Racing is not just a sport to me. It is the way I express discipline, identity and ambition. I started in karting at six years old, returned stronger after a forced break, and kept building through European karting, Ginetta Juniors and now GB4.

Off track, the project is bigger than a helmet and a car. It is a performance story, a Venezuelan story, a European racing story and a platform for brands that want to be part of something early, real and ambitious.

The Story Behind The Driver

Born From
Sacrifice &
Caracas

My mother is from Caracas, Venezuela. She left the country at 14 years old — alone, young, and determined — and made her way to Europe. That courage runs in my blood.

I was born into two worlds. I lived in Venezuela until I was three, then grew up in Belgium, always carrying that Venezuelan fire inside me. My family sacrifices every single day to keep this dream alive. Every lap I drive, I drive for them.

Motorsport in Venezuela has never had a voice at this level. That changes now. When I raise that flag on the podium, it is not just for me — it is for every Venezuelan kid who dares to dream big from a small place.
