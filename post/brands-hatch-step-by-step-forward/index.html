Brands Hatch:
Step by Step Forward

A weekend of learning, strong practice pace and hard racing at Brands Hatch.

Race Report
15 Oct 2025 · 1 min read

We went into the weekend full of confidence. Practice went great, ending P5 overall. The grid was tight and everyone was on the limit, so being that high up was a strong start.

Slipstream played a big role in qualifying again. I put together a clean lap, but without a tow it was difficult to match the front-runners. We qualified P15 for Race 1 and P13 for Race 2. Even then, the pace showed that with slipstream we could be much closer to the front.

Race 1 started with a mega launch off the line, straight into the top ten. The opening laps were intense. I fought hard but got caught in battles ahead, which allowed the chasing pack to close in. We finished P16, frustrating because the pace was there.

Race 2 was stopped after a big incident. There is not much to say apart from being glad everyone was alright.

Race 3 started P15 and finished P11. The race was messy, with red flags and interruptions, so we never fully got the chance to show our potential.

Overall, I learned a lot. Brands Hatch is one of those tracks I always dreamed of racing at, so finally doing it felt special. Despite the setbacks, the experience was incredible. We keep pushing.
